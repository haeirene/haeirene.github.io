Beyond orbits -

a tale of cosmic exploration

What is a year?

A year is a planetary orbit of Earth around the Sun while the Earth rotates 365 (or 366) times around its own axis. Movements that we cannot see with the naked eye, yet they are present in our daily lives. We move through life while planets move through space.

But what happens if you look at how other celestial bodies orbit? Unique patterns are formed, and they let us enjoy a refreshing perspective of our Solar System. ‘Beyond orbits’ shows these patterns and links them with how we simultaneously explore(d) our own home, the Solar System but also the universe. In this installation, the viewer is invited to time travel through space and (re)experience historical events in astronomy.
